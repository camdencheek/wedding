Our Story

We met in Grand Rapids Youth Symphony in early high school. Bekah dubbed Camden "the cute cello player" after Cam became stand partners a chair behind her best friend Courtney (maid of honor). Cam was too shy to introduce himself to Courtney, so his name stayed "cute cello player" until Camden and Bekah attended the same weeklong music camp a summer later and officially met through mutual friends. Fast forward a year or two of orchestra, and Bekah still thought Cam was cute, so she started talking to him on breaks at Youth Symphony. Over Christmas break senior year, she tried to schedule ice skating with a group of friends but nobody except Cam could join. She didn't mind, of course. Icy roads forced us to reschedule, but Cam's sister Kaedyn was sweet enough to let Cam miss part of her birthday celebration to join Bekah for ice skating and coffee. Cam finally got brave enough to ask Bekah on their first date on Valentine's Day 2014 (despite the show we went to being called "Rubies for Valentine's Day", Camden didn't realize he had scheduled our first date for Valentine's Day). We went out to dinner, then went to the ballet, then went back to Bekah's house to play cards. After a successful first date and a few after that, Bekah convinced Cam to make the relationship official on April 1, 2014 and the rest is history.

Thanks for reading! We're so blessed to have each of you in our lives and can't wait to share the beginning of our forever with all of you!
